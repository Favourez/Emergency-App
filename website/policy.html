Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 22, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Loope collects, uses, and protects your information.

What We Collect

  • Location (optional): Only when you tap "Signal an Emergency," we request access to your location to assist responders. We do not track or store it.
  • No Account Needed: We do not require users to create accounts or provide personal data.

Data Usage

Your data is used only for immediate emergency notification. We do not sell or share any user data.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect against misuse of your temporary location data.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Check back regularly for updates.

For questions, contact us at support@loope.app.
